# PaperVTT Vision

## Core Philosophy
PaperVTT is a **unified 3D world editor** that gives Game Masters the best of both worlds:

- The **speed and clarity** of a classic top-down 2D editor (like DungeonDraft)
- The **immersion and verticality** of a full 3D environment

All within the **same scene**. There is only one source of truth. Switching between perspectives is instant and seamless.

## The Dream Experience

You start in **TopDown2DView** — clean, orthographic, grid-snapped, and incredibly fast. You draw walls, place furniture, paint floors, and lay out rooms exactly like DungeonDraft. It feels familiar and productive.

You press **Tab**.

The camera smoothly tilts and orbits into **Free3DView**. Suddenly your flat top-down map becomes a rich, three-dimensional space. You can now sculpt hills, raise platforms, place objects on tables, hang lanterns from ceilings, adjust lighting, and walk around with a character in third-person.

You press **Tab** again — you’re instantly back in clean 2D mode, ready to continue building.

This cycle — 2D for speed, 3D for beauty and immersion — is the heart of PaperVTT.

## Key Pillars

### 1. Unified 3D World
- Everything exists in real 3D space at all times.
- Changes made in any view are immediately reflected everywhere.
- No duplicate data, no export/import steps, no synchronization headaches.

### 2. Perspective as a Tool
- **TopDown2DView**: Optimized for fast layout, precise placement, and DungeonDraft-style workflow.
- **Free3DView**: Optimized for vertical editing, atmospheric work, and playtesting.

### 3. Pen & Ink Identity
Strong, beautiful hand-drawn / graphic-novel styling is the default visual language. The world should feel like a living illustration that comes to life in 3D.

### 4. GM-First Design
Every feature is built for a solo GM who wants to create beautiful, usable maps quickly — then run immersive sessions in the same tool.

### 5. Future-Proof Foundation
Built so we can later add:
- Dynamic lighting & fog of war
- Full VTT multiplayer
- AI assistance
- Deep modding
- Narrative tools

## Success Statement

**When a GM opens PaperVTT, they should feel:**
> “This is exactly how I always wished I could build maps.”

They can work fast in 2D when they need to, dive into beautiful 3D when they want to, and never fight the tool.
